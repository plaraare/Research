How can we increase treatment selectivity against cancer?

One of key problems of metastasis is the lack of drugs able to target the small secondary tumor nodules.  My study  demonstrated that melanoma extracellular vesicles (EVs) act as natural nanoparticles and have a preferential uptake towards melanoma metastasis. The publication describes the potential of EVs as trojan horses for cancer therapeutics

Increasing antitumoral efficacy by reprograming cancer cells and their surrounding enviorment

Modulating the physiological state of EVs for optimal antitumoral efficacy is one of the key concepts of my research. In my research I evaluate the different antitumoral capacity of extracellular vesicles (EVs) according to their physiological states. For example, EVs from activated immune cells were able to induce Dendric cell maturation, reduce cell growth and promote a vaccine-like cancer immunological memory after PDT.

Using state of art technology for simultaneus treatment and imaging of cancer

Thanks to the funding obtained from the Dutch society for cancer control (KWF), I aim to develop a novel drug delivery platform using theranostic extracellular vesicles (EVs) for simultaneous immunotherapy, tumor targeting, photodynamic therapy (PDT) and imaging of cancer. To achieve this, a smart nanosystem is created using EVs, tumor antigens,  photosensitizers (PS) and upconversion nanoparticles (UCNPs) .

Using natural nanoparticles produced by our own cells to fight against cancer and other diseases

Extracelluar vesicles are natural nanoparticles, released by most cells which have great therapeutic potential. My research focus in developing  strategies to isolate this particules and modulate thier properties for targeting and immunotherapy

INCREASING THE THERAPEUTIC POTENTIAL OF PHOTODYNAMIC THERAPY

Photodynamic therapy (PDT) is a promising and clinically approved method for the treatment of cancer. However, the efficacy of PDT is often limited by the poor selectivity and distribution of the photosensitizers (PS) toward the malignant tumors, resulting in prolonged periods of skin photosensitivity.

I developed a strategy to increase the tumor distribution, selectivity, and efficacy of lipophilic PS by their stabilization in purified, naturally secreted extracellular vesicles (EVs)

TREATING CANCER BY EXTERNAL LIGHT IRRADIATION

We aim to control by external stimulation, the release of drugs that are co-encapsulated with metallic nanoparticles. In this way, the management and control of these systems will considerably improve therapeutic efficiency and reduce adverse and collateral effects, resulting in greater acceptance by patients.

NANOFIX: ​SCIENTIFIC STUDIES & TECHNOLOGICAL DEVELOPMENT

 

There are more than 15 thousand scientific studies that prove the functionality of curcumin. Only 3% of a kilo of turmeric corresponds to Curcumin, the rest does not have scientifically proven properties.  Furthermore, the oral bioavailability of curcumin is low due to a relatively low absorption by small intestine
At Nanofix we take this laboratory technology and bring it to a simple and easy-to-take food product, managing to solubilize the molecules and transport the nanoparticles in a unique nanometer-sized vehicle (<220 nanometers) to achieve the maximum absorption today available on the market. Download our Study ( Study published in Nanoscale Magazine ) which led to this innovation.
